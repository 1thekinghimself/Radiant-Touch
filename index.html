Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas in Lagos do you serve?

We serve all major areas in Lagos including Ikeja, Victoria Island, Lekki, Ikoyi, Surulere, Yaba, and surrounding neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies, equipment, and eco-friendly products. However, if you prefer us to use your specific products, we're happy to accommodate that request.

How do you determine pricing?

Pricing is based on the size of the space, type of cleaning required, and frequency of service. We provide transparent quotes with no hidden fees after assessing your specific needs.

Are your cleaners insured and background checked?

Absolutely. All our cleaners undergo thorough background checks, are fully insured, and receive comprehensive training to ensure the highest quality service and your peace of mind.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel or reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours notice without any charge. Last-minute cancellations may incur a small fee.
